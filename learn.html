Universal Protection Tips

These rules protect you against nearly every type of scam

Stop & Verify

Any unsolicited contact asking for money or information — stop, hang up, and verify through official channels.

Never Pay with Gift Cards

No legitimate organization — government, bank, or business — ever asks for payment via gift cards. Ever.

Verify URLs Before Clicking

Hover over links to see the real destination. Type URLs directly into your browser rather than clicking links.

Enable 2FA Everywhere

Two-factor authentication blocks 99% of automated account takeover attacks even if passwords are stolen.

Guard Your SSN

Your Social Security Number is the master key to your identity. Share it only when legally required.

Talk About It

Scammers thrive on secrecy. Discuss suspicious contacts with a trusted friend or family member before acting.
