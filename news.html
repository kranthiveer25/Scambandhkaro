UPI & Cyber Fraud Cost India ₹11,333 Crore in 2025 — MHA Report

UPI Fraud KYC Scam March 18, 2026 7 min read

A Ministry of Home Affairs cybercrime report reveals India lost over ₹11,333 crore to online fraud in 2025 — with UPI payment fraud, fake KYC calls, and WhatsApp job scams as the top three categories. Over 1.2 million complaints were filed on the National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal (cybercrime.gov.in). The Cyber Crime Helpline 1930 received 7 lakh calls. Experts urge citizens to never share OTP, always verify QR codes, and report fraud within 24 hours for the best chance of recovery.
