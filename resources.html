Immediate Help — If You've Been Scammed

If you've lost money or shared personal information with a scammer, act immediately:

Contact Your Bank

Call the number on the back of your card immediately to report fraud and freeze your accounts.

Report to FTC

File a fraud report at ReportFraud.ftc.gov. It fuels law enforcement action.

Freeze Your Credit

Contact Equifax, Experian, and TransUnion to place a credit freeze if your SSN was shared.

Change Passwords

Immediately change passwords on all accounts, especially email and financial accounts.
